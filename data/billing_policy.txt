Billing Policy

Overview:
Our billing system posts charges after 24 hours of service usage. Customers may see estimated costs in the dashboard before final settlement.

Duplicate charge review:
If a customer reports duplicate charges, support should verify the order ID, transaction ID, and billing cycle. Refunds may be issued when duplicate invoices are confirmed.

Refund requests:
Refunds are handled within 5 working days. Pending refunds are subject to account validation and may require additional verification from the billing team.
